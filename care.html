My Basic Care Steps

Water tracking

A huge portion of taking care of plants relies on a consistent watering schedule that meets the plants needs and takes into consideration the enviroment the plant lives in. I... cheat... and use an app called "Planta" to track each of my plants' watering needs. The app will remind me when to water, mist, fertilize, repot, and will even diagnose health issues with each of my plants. I really only like to use the watering schedule to assist in my care, but will use the health check feature from time to time.

Pot/Soil Changing

As far as repotting and chaing the plant soil goes, its more of a gut feeling. There is probably good science behind knowing when to change the soil or pot, but I prefer to rely on my gut to make the decision. If a plant is starting to stay stagnate, it's heatlh is declining or I notice roots growing from the bottom, I know it's time to change the pot and/or soil.

Light

I have yet to figure out lighting for my plants. I utilize a grow light for the majority of my plants because I am simply too chicken to put the plants outside in the dry and warm Arizona climate. Each time I have tried, even with "Arizona" plants, the plant typically widdles away. It's too sad to see, so I stunt their growth for the safety of an indoor grow light. The plus side is I get to see them all the time!
