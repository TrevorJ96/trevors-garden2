Welcome!

Welcome to my little home garden. On this website I have pictures, names, and descriptions of each of the plants I am currently taking care of to the best of my ability. Some have died along the way, some have not looked the best, but I certainly try my best to take care of them.

I got into plants about four years ago now when my then girlfriend, now fiance, got me a silly potted plant with a face on it. Since then, I have grown my plant collection to nine plants under my care. I have certainly lost a few along the way unfortunately, but several have been with me for a while.

In my website here I have the current plants under my care, future plant plans for when I have a backyard or more space, a little bit of background on the species in the garden, how I take care of the plants, and a few timeline photos of the plants along the way.
